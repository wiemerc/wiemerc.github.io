About me

Translations: de

Hello and welcome to my blog! My name is Constantin Wiemer and I'm writing here about my programming projects, computer security and once in a while maybe even about computer history. Currently I'm interested in systems programming in C and sometimes assembly and learning how certain kinds of exploits work in detail ("how hackers break into systems"). Apart from the projects I wrote about here, I also wrote stuff like a linker for the long-forgotten AmigaOS or a TFTP server that uses ICMP as transport protocol.

I'm a system administrator and software developer by trade, with stints in database administration, the world of networks (at a mobile operator) and sometimes even project management. Although it seems to me that nowadays specialists are more in demand, I've always been an all-rounder and I like it that way. I tried to show in a mindmap all the technologies I have experience with. At the moment I work as a devops engineer, software developer and technical lead for a large insurance company in the application monitoring field.

I'm looking forward to comments and questions and can be reached at constantin.wiemer@gmx.de.
